# 🏢 Business Problem

The organization lacked an integrated performance monitoring system across Finance, Sales, Marketing, and Executive operations.

Decision-makers required:
- Consolidated KPI tracking
- Profitability visibility
- Market share insights
- Regional & customer-level performance monitoring
- Risk identification (Inventory / Forecast issues)

---

# 🎯 Project Objective

To develop a multi-page 360° Business Intelligence dashboard enabling leadership to:

- Monitor financial performance (Revenue, GM, Net Profit)
- Analyze product & customer profitability
- Track market share trends vs competitors
- Evaluate regional & channel performance
- Identify risk areas (Out-of-Stock / Excess Inventory)
- Improve strategic decision-making

---

# 🧩 Dashboard Architecture

### 1️⃣ Executive View
- Revenue by Division
- Revenue by Channel
- Market Share Trend vs Competitors
- Forecast Accuracy
- Sub-Zone Risk Insights

### 2️⃣ Finance View
- Profit & Loss Statement
- Net Sales Trend
- GM % and Net Profit Analysis
- Top/Bottom Products & Customers

### 3️⃣ Sales View
- Product Performance (NS, GM, GM%)
- Customer Performance Matrix
- Regional Performance Scatter Analysis
- Revenue Contribution Analysis

### 4️⃣ Marketing View
- Net Profit % Performance Matrix
- Unit Economics (GM → Expenses → Net Profit)
- Segment-wise Profitability
- Region / Market / Customer breakdown

---

# 📊 Key KPIs Designed

- Net Sales
- Gross Margin (GM $ & %)
- Net Profit ($ & %)
- Revenue Contribution %
- Forecast Accuracy %
- Risk Classification
- Market Share %
- WoW / YoY Change %

---

# 📈 Strategic Insights

- Revenue concentration observed in select divisions
- Certain high-revenue segments show negative net profit
- Regional profitability varies significantly (APAC vs EU vs NA)
- Market share growth influenced by competitor dynamics
- Inventory risks identified across specific sub-zones
- Forecast variance impacting profit margins
